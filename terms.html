TERMS

Rules for a vulnerable public archive

The archive works only when people can write honestly without harming others. These rules keep the project readable, safe, and worth returning to.

Allowed

Personal reflections, apologies, confessions, grief, unresolved memories, and emotionally honest writing that does not target someone with abuse or threats.

Not allowed

Harassment, doxxing, threats, hate speech, explicit personal data, spam, and messages that turn the archive into a tool for public humiliation.

Moderation

Content may be hidden or removed if it creates risk for a real person, violates privacy, or clearly breaks the tone and purpose of the project.

Privacy

Keep messages anonymous by default and avoid including identifying details. The strongest posts are usually the most emotionally precise, not the most specific.

Write clearly, stay anonymous, and leave room for other people to remain human inside the archive.
